Guru Nanak Institutions Technical Campus (GNITC) is the world class educational institution located at the Rangareddy district of the Telangana state. Two constituent units of GNITC are: Guru Nanak Engineering College (GNEC) and Guru Nanak Institute of Engineering and Technology. This institute promotes the learning in the fields of engineering and technology, management, pharmacy and computer applications. The institute is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to the JNTU, Hyderabad.

CAMPUS

Guru Nanak Institutions Technical Campus covers the prime area of 50 acres of land which holds academic building blocks, separate playground, lawns and greeneries. The campus is only 28 kms far from the Hyderabad city and fully connected by roads.

FACILITIES

The students of GNITC are provided with the hostel facility with basic amenities of safe drinking water, entertainment services, autonomous library for hostel students, 24 hours internet facility, warm water for bathing, fooding services, indoor and outdoor sport blocks. The campus has the prime digital library for all students; well furnished guest rooms are also available within the campus, banking and ATM services in the campus and many more.
